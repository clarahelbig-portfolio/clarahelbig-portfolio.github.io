Ocean Noise ( Audio 2018, CGI Animation ongoing)

Almost 95% of the oceans remain unexplored by humanity, yet anthropogenic noise pollution is exponentially increasing its impact on marine life forms. Ocean Noise explores the consequences of humans altering aquatic sound scapes.
